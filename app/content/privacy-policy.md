# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated**: November 7, 2024  
**Version**: 2.1

## 1. Introduction

At Innovative Finance Limited ("Innovative Finance," "we," "us," or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, visit our website, or interact with us through our mobile applications and other digital platforms.

This policy complies with the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

#### Identity Information
- Full name and contact details (address, phone number, email)
- Date of birth and national identification information (Ghana Card, Passport, Voter ID)
- Photographs for identity verification
- Digital signatures and biometric data

#### Financial Information
- Employment and income information
- Bank account details and financial statements
- Credit history and financial information
- Transaction history and banking behavior
- Tax identification numbers

#### Application Data
- Loan application details and supporting documents
- Business information for commercial clients
- Educational qualifications and professional certifications
- References and guarantor information

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect:

#### Technical Information
- Device information (type, model, operating system)
- IP addresses and browser information
- Location data (with your consent)
- Usage patterns and app performance metrics

#### Behavioral Data
- Website and app navigation patterns
- Service preferences and settings
- Communication preferences
- Login times and session duration

### 2.3 Third-Party Sources

We may collect information about you from:

- Credit reference agencies and bureaus
- Identity verification services
- Government databases and registries
- Partner financial institutions
- Employer verification services
- Social media platforms (with your consent)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Provision
- **Account Management**: Opening, maintaining, and managing your accounts
- **Transaction Processing**: Executing payments, transfers, and other banking transactions
- **Loan Services**: Assessing loan applications and managing existing loans
- **Customer Support**: Providing assistance and responding to inquiries

### 3.2 Legal and Regulatory Compliance
- **Know Your Customer (KYC)**: Verifying customer identity and meeting regulatory requirements
- **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**: Detecting and preventing financial crimes
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Complying with Bank of Ghana and other regulatory requirements
- **Tax Compliance**: Meeting tax reporting obligations

### 3.3 Risk Management and Security
- **Credit Assessment**: Evaluating creditworthiness and loan applications
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities
- **Security Monitoring**: Protecting against unauthorized access and cyber threats
- **Debt Collection**: Managing overdue accounts and collections

### 3.4 Business Operations
- **Product Development**: Improving existing services and developing new products
- **Marketing and Communications**: Sending relevant offers and service updates
- **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns and improving customer experience
- **Research**: Conducting market research and analysis

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

### 4.1 Contractual Necessity
- Performing our obligations under banking and loan agreements
- Processing transactions and managing accounts
- Providing customer support and services

### 4.2 Legal Obligation
- Complying with banking regulations and laws
- Meeting KYC/AML requirements
- Regulatory reporting and tax compliance

### 4.3 Legitimate Interest
- Fraud prevention and security
- Product development and improvement
- Direct marketing (with opt-out options)
- Business analytics and research

### 4.4 Consent
- Location-based services
- Marketing communications
- Biometric data processing
- Third-party integrations

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

### 5.1 Service Providers
- **Technology Partners**: Cloud storage, software, and IT services
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction processing and payment networks
- **Identity Verification**: KYC and identity verification services
- **Customer Support**: Third-party customer service providers

### 5.2 Regulatory and Legal Authorities
- **Bank of Ghana**: Regulatory reporting and compliance
- **Ghana Revenue Authority**: Tax reporting and compliance
- **Law Enforcement**: When required by law or court order
- **Financial Intelligence Centre**: AML and suspicious transaction reporting

### 5.3 Business Partners
- **Credit Reference Agencies**: Credit reporting and assessment
- **Insurance Companies**: For loan protection and insurance products
- **Partner Banks**: For correspondent banking services
- **Merchants**: For payment processing services

### 5.4 Corporate Transactions
- **Mergers and Acquisitions**: In case of business restructuring
- **Asset Sales**: When selling portions of our business
- **Joint Ventures**: For collaborative business initiatives

## 6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
- **Access Controls**: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access restrictions
- **Network Security**: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and monitoring systems
- **Regular Updates**: Continuous security patching and system updates

### 6.2 Physical Security
- **Secure Data Centers**: Restricted access facilities with surveillance
- **Device Security**: Secure workstations and mobile device management
- **Document Security**: Secure storage and disposal of physical documents

### 6.3 Administrative Controls
- **Staff Training**: Regular security awareness and privacy training
- **Background Checks**: Thorough screening of employees and contractors
- **Incident Response**: Comprehensive breach response procedures
- **Regular Audits**: Internal and external security assessments

### 6.4 Monitoring and Detection
- **24/7 Monitoring**: Continuous surveillance of systems and networks
- **Anomaly Detection**: AI-powered fraud and security threat detection
- **Log Management**: Comprehensive audit trails and log analysis
- **Vulnerability Testing**: Regular penetration testing and security assessments

## 7. International Data Transfers

When transferring data internationally, we ensure:

- **Adequacy Decisions**: Transfers only to countries with adequate data protection
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: Using EU-approved contractual protections
- **Certification Programs**: Working with certified service providers
- **Regular Reviews**: Ongoing assessment of transfer mechanisms

## 8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

### 8.1 Access Rights
- **Data Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Processing Information**: Understand how we use your information
- **Source Information**: Know where we obtained your data
- **Sharing Details**: Learn who we share your data with

### 8.2 Correction and Update
- **Data Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Data Completion**: Provide additional information where incomplete
- **Update Preferences**: Change your communication preferences

### 8.3 Deletion Rights
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Retention Review**: Challenge our data retention decisions
- **Account Closure**: Delete data upon account termination

### 8.4 Processing Restrictions
- **Object to Processing**: Opt out of certain data processing activities
- **Marketing Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Profiling Limits**: Restrict automated decision-making

### 8.5 Data Portability
- **Data Export**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Transfer Assistance**: Help transferring data to another service provider

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your information based on:

### 9.1 Retention Periods
- **Active Accounts**: Throughout the relationship and for regulatory requirements
- **Closed Accounts**: Up to 10 years after account closure
- **Loan Records**: 15 years after loan completion
- **Transaction Records**: 7 years for audit and regulatory purposes

### 9.2 Retention Criteria
- **Legal Requirements**: Compliance with banking and financial regulations
- **Business Needs**: Ongoing customer relationship and service provision
- **Legitimate Interests**: Fraud prevention and risk management
- **Data Minimization**: Regular review and deletion of unnecessary data

### 10.1 Cookie Types
- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for website functionality
- **Performance Cookies**: Analytics and website improvement
- **Functional Cookies**: Enhanced features and personalization
- **Marketing Cookies**: Targeted advertising and marketing

### 10.2 Cookie Management
- **Browser Settings**: Control cookies through browser preferences
- **Opt-out Tools**: Use industry opt-out mechanisms
- **Cookie Policy**: Detailed information in our Cookie Policy

## 11. Marketing and Communications

### 11.1 Marketing Consent
We may send you marketing communications if:
- You have given explicit consent
- You are an existing customer and the marketing relates to similar services
- We have a legitimate interest (with easy opt-out options)

### 11.2 Communication Channels
- **Email**: Service updates and promotional offers
- **SMS**: Account alerts and marketing messages
- **Phone**: Customer service and marketing calls
- **Push Notifications**: Mobile app notifications

### 11.3 Opt-out Options
- **Email Unsubscribe**: Links in all marketing emails
- **SMS Stop**: Text "STOP" to opt out of SMS marketing
- **Account Settings**: Manage preferences through online banking
- **Customer Service**: Contact us to update preferences

- **Educational Programs**: Financial literacy for young adults

## 13. Privacy by Design

We incorporate privacy principles into:

- **Product Development**: Privacy considerations in new service design

- **Regular Assessments**: Privacy impact assessments for new initiatives

## 14. Complaint Resolution

If you have concerns about our privacy practices:

### 14.1 Internal Process
1. **Contact Us**: Reach out to our Data Protection Officer
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate your concerns promptly
3. **Response**: Receive a written response within 30 days
4. **Resolution**: Implement appropriate remedial actions

### 14.2 External Remedies
- **Data Protection Commission**: File a complaint with Ghana's data protection authority
- **Legal Action**: Pursue legal remedies through the courts
- **Industry Associations**: Contact relevant banking ombudsman services
